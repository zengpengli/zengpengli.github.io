Hi, I am currently an associate professor with the School of Cyber Science and Technology at Shandong University (SDU) Qingdao Campus and supported by the SDU Young Scholars Future Program. Prior to joining SDU, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Singapore University of Technology & Design (SUTD). I worked with Dr. Paweł Szałachowski and Prof. Jianying Zhou on post-quantum distributed computing, e.g., blockchain consensus protocol. Prior to joining SUTD, I was a postdoctoral research associate at Lancaster University from Jan. 2018 worked with Dr. Bingsheng Zhang and Dr. Jan Willemson on cryptographic e-voting and zero-knowledge proof, etc. My postdoctoral works with my advisors are one part of Cybernetica and IOHK.

Before that, I passed my oral defense in Dec. 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Chunguang Ma. During my doctoral program, I have been very fortunate to have worked with Prof. Steven Galbraith and Prof. Hong-Sheng Zhou, respectively, as a research assistant (and a joint Ph.D. program student) from Dec. 2015 - Oct. 2017.

Research Interests: My primary research interests are cryptographic protocols, secure distributed computing, and privacy-enhancing technologies for real-world applications. Recently, specific topics include: computing and query on encrypted data, verifiable computation in outsourced environments, and password-based cryptography.

Please don't hesitate to email me if you are interested in my research. I'm looking for passionate and motivated students to drive my projects. (Basically, I could provide 0-2 doctoral student positions and 3-4 master's student positions from 2022.)
